  Challenges in Extractly.ai

A product suite designed for accounting firms to audit financial statements and other documents.

Extractly.ai – Transforming the Audit Process

Extractly.ai is a SaaS platform that modernizes traditional auditing by making it more streamlined, automated, and efficient. Its product suite includes Validate and Extractly+.

My key contributions:

  • Built a comprehensive design system – Established branding and UI components from the ground up, ensuring consistency and scalability.

  • Optimized design processes – Developed a tailored workflow to maximize efficiency within our team’s resources.

  • Led cross-functional collaboration – Educated stakeholders and worked closely with developers to integrate user-centered design principles.

  • Drove product innovation – Helped launch Extractly+ from concept to market within six months, expanding our product suite.

Today, Extractly.ai continues to evolve, offering a growing range of tools that enhance the audit process for customers across Australia, New Zealand, and the UK.

Challenge 1: No Design Process in Place

What I have done :

the development of a design cycle

  • Established a structured design cycle to bring clarity and efficiency to the project.

  • Educated stakeholders through company-wide meetings and requirement-gathering sessions, ensuring alignment and engagement.

  • Identified key pain points in the development process, fostering a culture of proactive problem-solving.

  • Incorporated user feedback to validate design decisions and prioritize user needs.

  • Developed a tailored design process that optimized resources, improving efficiency and collaboration.

Challenge 2: Limited Style Guide & Resources

What I have done :

The development of a design system

  • Developed a full-fledged design system to expand beyond a basic style guide, incorporating colors, grids, icons, typography, components, and input fields for a cohesive design language.

  • Advocated for the design system’s value, highlighting its role in improving efficiency, enabling reusable components, and ensuring brand consistency.

  • Optimized Figma workflows to enhance collaboration and scalability, continuously refining the system for better usability and maintainability.

 

Challenge 3: Inclusive and accessible design

What I have done :

Be mindful of the inclusive design

  • Ensured accessibility compliance by meeting or exceeding WCAG 2.0 Level AA standards for color contrast.

  • Improved readability by refining typography and layout for better visibility and user-friendly navigation.

  • Enhanced differentiation by redesigning markup patterns beyond traditional color cues, making the product more inclusive for all users.
